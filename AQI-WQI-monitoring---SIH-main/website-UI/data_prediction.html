Prediction Choice

Predict Air Quality Index (AQI)


The Air Quality Index (AQI) is a standardized measurement system that provides information about the quality of the air we breathe. This index serves as a vital tool for assessing and communicating air quality conditions to the public, helping individuals make informed decisions to protect their health and the environment.

Predict Water Quality Index (WQI)


The Water Quality Index (WQI) is a numerical rating system used to assess the overall quality of water in a particular area. WQI provides valuable information about the safety and suitability of water for various purposes, including drinking, recreation, and ecological health, helping to guide water resource management and protection efforts.
