Our Services

Data Monitoring

We have implemented a comprehensive data monitoring system for air and water quality in the designated region, offering accessible APIs. Our sophisticated algorithms enable real-time calculation of both Air Quality Index (AQI) and Water Quality Index (WQI), providing concerned citizens, organization and government sectors with valuable insights to maintain a healthier and more sustainable environment.

Data Prediction

Utilizing a validated dataset and a robust machine learning model, we are well-equipped to predict future Air Quality Index (AQI) and Water Quality Index (WQI) with a high degree of accuracy. Our rigorous approach ensures the reliability of these predictions, empowering informed decision-making and proactive environmental management.
